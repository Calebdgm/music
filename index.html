Caleb deGroot-Maggetti

Caleb deGroot-Maggetti is a pianist, concertina player, and music teacher. He's based in the city of Kitchener (in Ontario, Canada), which is the traditional territory of the Haudenosaunee, the Anishinaabe, and the Neutral peoples.

Experience

Caleb has been playing piano for church services since 2017 and is currently the music director at Church of the Good Shepherd in Kitchener. He has performed at the Uptown Waterloo Jazz Festival with the Iron Tonic Jazz Band in 2019 and 2022. He has also performed in local bars with the Uptown Sound Jazz Collective, and at local porch parties with his family band, Fiddle ‘n’ Swing.

Teaching

Caleb has taught piano to children and adults since 2016. For more information, see the Teaching page.
