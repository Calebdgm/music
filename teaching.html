Learning Piano with Caleb

Teacher Bio

Caleb has taught piano to children and adults since 2017. He’s a patient and adaptable teacher who works with students to meet their goals and strives to show them a range of possibilities for what they can learn. He believes that the practice of piano can and should be fun.

Lesson Details

  • Fee: $30 for a 30-minute lesson, $60 for a 1-hour lesson
  • Lessons are booked weekly and paid by the month (so 4 or 5 weeks at a time)
  • Location: 116 Queen St N, Kitchener ON, or ask about online lessons
  • Email Caleb at Calebdgm@gmail.com for more information or to book a first lesson. Or use the contact form below.
